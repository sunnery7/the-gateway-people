The authorities of Tai Solarin Federal University of Education (TASFUED), Ijagun, Ogun State has hailed its 17th convocation overall best graduate, Bukola Ifeoluwa Osunrinde.
The student, distinguished and achieved a perfect Calculative Grade Point Aggregate (CGPA) of 5.0.
Osunrinde will be formally recognized, honoured and celebrated on the convocation day, January 29, 2026.
During the ceremony, three exceptional Nigerians – the First Lady, Senator Oluremi Tinubu, Governor Dapo Abiodun and former governor of Ogun State, Aremo Olusegun Osoba, will also be bestowed with the awards of Honorary Doctor of Science (D.Sc., honours causa).
Acting Vice-Chancellor, Prof. Adekunle Olanrewaju Adeogun, gave the commendation at the weekend during a press briefing at the Senate Chamber to mark the 17th convocation ceremony.

Prof. Adeogun revealed that the university produced a total of 4,785 graduates from the 2024/2025 set of regular students.

A breakdown of the graduates showed that 4 obtained First Class Honours, 1,466 obtained Second Class Upper Division,
2,768  made Second Class Lower Division while 492 and 19 obtained Third Class Pass Degrees respectively.
