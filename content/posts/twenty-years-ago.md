Former Ogun State Deputy Governor, Alhaja Salmat Badru at Lisa Village, on October 22, 2005 when the Belview Airline crashed killing all passengers on board. Surrounding the governor are government officials including the current Head of Service, Mr. Kehinde Onasanya, then Press Officer to the then governor,  Otunba Gbenga Daniel.
