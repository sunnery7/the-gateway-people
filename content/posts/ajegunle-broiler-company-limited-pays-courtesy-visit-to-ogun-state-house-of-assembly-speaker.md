In a formal engagement aimed at fostering stronger collaboration between the private sector and government.


The Ajegunle Broiler Company Limited (ABCL), a key agricultural enterprise based in the Ajegunle Farm Settlement, Odeda Local Government Area of Ogun State paid a courtesy call on Rt. Hon. Daisi Olusegun Victor Elemide, Speaker of the State House of Assembly.


 The visit led by its chairman, Mr. Rotimi Sogunle, provided an opportunity for both parties to discuss potential areas of co-operation and support towards regional agricultural development and economic growth.


The delegation  expressed their commitment to sectoral advancement, while the speaker welcomed the initiative, emphasizing the importance of synergy between legislative frameworks and business operations. 
In his remarks, Hon. Elemide, who commended the initiative of the ABCL emphasized the importance of synergy between legislative frameworks and business operations to support agricultural development and economic growth in the state. 


He welcomed the visit as a positive step towards building stronger public-private partnerships for sustainable development.


The speaker praised the company for its engagement, highlighted the need for government and business to work together, and endorsed the collaboration as beneficial for the state's progress.


"This visit underscores the ongoing efforts to strengthen public-private partnerships for sustainable development in Ogun State", he stated
